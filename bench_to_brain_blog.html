About Me

I'm Krithika Rajesh, a passionate researcher with a Master's in Human Genetics and a deep commitment to unraveling the mysteries of neurodegeneration—especially Parkinson's disease. With a foundation in stem cell biology and regenerative medicine, I aim to bridge the gap between fundamental research and clinical therapies. My experience spans ADMSC culture, senescence studies, PCC induction, and disease modeling, fueled by both academic rigor and personal drive. This blog is a space to share insights, breakthroughs, and reflections on the journey from petri dish to patient care.
